05 / How We Operate

Embedded, not
at arm's length.

You're not handing work to a vendor. We run on your stack and inside your workflow, exactly how we run NationGraph's media function today.

Planning
A shared Notion hub: editorial calendar, shoot plans, scripts, and an asset tracker where every approved piece is one click to re-share.
Communication
A shared Slack Connect channel, weekly sync, and monthly readout with the Director of Brand and Creative Director. Day-to-day, async.
Content Review
Every cut lands in Frame.io with timestamped notes. Feedback in context, on your own time, no email threads.

Final files in an organized Dropbox that's yours, an archive that compounds month over month.
Artlist

On-site
On the ground in NYC for every shoot, planned around your team, operator-site and event travel included. Everything else runs remotely.
